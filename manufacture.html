The manufacturing industry must accept its heavy responsibility for emissions of greenhouse gases. In the US, manufacturing accounts for almost a quarter (23%) of direct carbon emissions, according to the Environmental Protection Agency.

Producing computers uses up lots of energy. This adds to climate change because fossil fuels are burned to create the energy. 70 per cent of the energy a typical laptop will consume during its lifespan is used in manufacturing the computer: 227�270 kilograms (or 500�594 pounds) of CO2 are emitted in manufacturing a laptop computer.
